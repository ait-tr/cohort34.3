Валидация email:
  1) @ exists and only one
  2) dot after @
  3) after last dot minimum 2 symbols
  4) alphabetic, digits, _ , - , . , @

Валидация password:
  1) min 8 symbols
  2) min one symbol of uppercase
  3) min one symbol of lowercase
  4) min one digit
  5) min one special symbol (!%@*&)
